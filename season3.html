Season 3

New threats emerge from the conflict, further disrupting the tenuous balance of power. In addition to assassins, bounty hunters, monsters and more, Season Three pits the Jedi Knights against Jabba the Hutt, Greedo and the deadly brother of Sith apprentice Darth Maul! Mystery, intrigue and adventure await Padawan Ahsoka Tano as she must prove her strength as a Jedi with the help of a familiar furry ally, Chewbacca.
